About Truck Service Hub

Truck Service Hub is India's most reliable digital platform for truck booking and logistics. Our goal is to connect users with available truck types such as pickups, eicher trucks, containers, trailers, and parcel services, all in real-time.

With cutting-edge technology, we make truck availability visible to customers and help transporters increase business efficiency. Whether you're transporting goods for your company or need part-load delivery services, we’ve got your back.

Why Choose Us?

  • Verified and real-time available trucks
  • Coverage across all major Indian cities
  • Affordable, transparent pricing
  • Live location tracking & responsive customer support
